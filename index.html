Overall info

This is a game that most users like. It is made for all kinds of creative people. Boys, girls and everything in-between. Minecraft is a video game in which players create and break apart various kinds of blocks in three-dimensional worlds. The game's two main modes are Survival and Creative. In Survival, players must find their own building supplies and food. They also interact with blocklike mobs, or moving creatures.
